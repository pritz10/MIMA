About Me

Pravesh Tamang aka Chozel

Art Director, Music In Motion Dance Academy

Born on March 17, 1995

Pravesh Tamang is a dynamic artist, educator, and social advocate, born in Sikkim, India. He has made remarkable contributions to the dance community and has been a prominent figure in shaping the dance culture in his region.

Education & Training
  • B.Com Honors degree from Sikkim University

  • Diploma in Performing Theatre Arts from APG Goyal Shimla University (2021)

  • International Professional Dance Study Program from Prince Gupta Fame of Dance India Dance Institute, Delhi
Career Highlights
  • Art Director at Music In Motion Dance Academy, founded in 2014

  • Winner, Solo Category Freestyle Dance at International Groove Fest 2023, Colombo, Sri Lanka, and recipient of the Shining Star Award

  • Represented Sikkim in various national and international hip-hop dance events

  • Invited as a national judge and guest instructor at international dance camps

  • Finalist of Yes I’m India’s Biggest Reality Show at Goregaon Film City, Mumbai
Personal Interests & Philanthropy
  • Running, swimming, hiking, cycling, and basketball

  • Committed to social work, with a passion for supporting the underprivileged and nurturing young talent.
Motto

    "Never stop learning, because life never stops teaching."
